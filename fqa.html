Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?

You can either pay cash on delivery or by using a credit card during the checkout process.

How much is Vicky's Pizza's delivery fee?

$5.00 fee for all deliveries. Contactless delivery is provided at standard delivery costs for any customer who would prefer this option.

Is my payment information secure?

All credit card data is securely encrypted and transmitted. Your credit card information is captured, and it is never shared with the restaurant.

When is my credit card charged?

Credit cards are authorized immediately, but we wait 6 hours to process payments to account for any changes. Any adjustments made after 6 hours will appear as a separate charge or refund.

How do promotion codes work?

If you receive a promotion code, you need to enter it upon checkout to have the value deducted from your order total. Please, be sure to check the terms and conditions of your code.

What happens after I place my order?

We’ll send you a confirmation email with delivery or pickup time estimations.

How can I edit my order?

Please contact the pizzeria as soon as possible, using the phone number found in your confirmation email and on this website. We’ll do our best to edit your order.

How can I cancel my order?

Please, contact the pizzeria by phone as soon as possible. We’ll do our best to cancel your order, but if the order is already being cooked, we may not be able to approve a cancellation.
