Our Story

The Vicky's Pizza Company proudly serves its customers mouth-watering thin crust European style Pizza on a delicious sesame crust.

We serve a unique range of ten savoury and two sweet Pizzas using our top-secret proprietary dough recipe. We mix and round this each day, blend our own cheese and sauces..

Opening in 2018 and working tirelessly to build our customer base in the Central City we are thrilled to serve you and hope you will enjoy your pizza as much as we enjoy making it.
Catering, in house or via delivery we will be there for you every day. With two stores in New Zealand and many more to follow we are looking forward to becoming a staple whenever Kiwis look for a quality Pizza experience.
Join us on our journey as we open more restaurants around our beautiful country. We look forward to being your pizza place!.
